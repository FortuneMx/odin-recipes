Victoria Sandwich Cake

Description

Victoria sponge cake, a much loved English layer cake, is perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 160g flour
  • 2 level tsp. baking powder
  • 120g margarine
  • 120g caster sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2-3 drops vanilla essence
  • cold water to mix
  • 2 tblsp. smooth jam

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease 2 sandwich cake tins with oil or unsalted fat and dredge with a little flour.
  2. Cream margarine and sugar with essence.
  3. Sieve flour and baking powder together onto a plate.
  4. Lightly beat eggs in a small basin.
  5. Add egg gradually to the creamed mixture, beating well between each addition (a little flour may be added to prevent curdling).
  6. When all the egg has been added, cease beating and fold in the remainder of the flour a little at a time. Add cold water to keep mixture of dropping consistency.
  7. Divide mixture equally between the two tins and spread lightly.
  8. Bake 30–40 minutes. When cooked, the cakes should be well risen, golden brown, and slightly shrunken from sides of tins.
  9. Cakes should respond to the fingertip test.
  10. Allow cakes to cool completely on a cooling rack, and then sandwich the two together with the jam. Dredge the top with fine sugar.

Variations

For Chocolate Sandwich Cake , mix 2 level tblsp. cocoa with 2 tblsp. boiling water and add to mixture. Sandwich together with butter cream.
